Royalty-at-risk figures estimate uncollected mechanical royalties, priced with commonly-cited per-stream mechanical rates (Spotify $0.000393, Apple Music $0.000456, Audiomack $0.00012). Platforms tagged real use measured per-platform stream counts (via Soundcharts, when configured — it covers Spotify streams and Audiomack plays); platforms tagged est. are estimated from the measured data by market share (Apple Music publishes no play counts). With no measured data, the total stream count (real or projected) is split by a modeled Afro-market distribution (Spotify 45% / Apple 25% / Audiomack 30%). Figures illustrate downstream impact, not guaranteed amounts. CMO registration is checked against a demo registry; production would query SOCAN / ASCAP / MCSK directly. The interactive tool lets you adjust each platform's streams (and add more platforms) to model growth; added-platform rates are rough estimates you can tune.
