Royalty-at-risk figures estimate uncollected mechanical royalties. The total stream count (real or projected) is split across platforms by a modeled market-share distribution (Spotify 45% / Apple Music 25% / Audiomack 30%, Afro-market tilt), then priced with commonly-cited per-stream mechanical rates (Spotify $0.000393, Apple Music $0.000456, Audiomack $0.00012). No free/official API exposes real per-platform play counts, so the split is a defensible model, not measured data — plug in real per-artist numbers to refine it. Figures illustrate downstream impact, not guaranteed amounts. CMO registration is checked against a demo registry; production would query SOCAN / ASCAP / MCSK directly.
